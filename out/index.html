Welcome

You have landed at ssblur.com, now simpler than ever. Navigate to This Site to learn more about the design decisions that went into the making of this website.

I'm blur. Sometimes people call me SSBlur, blub, biub, or some dozens of other derivatives of these names.

Some questions I get asked:

  • Q: What do you do?
    • In my day job, I work as a software engineer on DevOps and testing tools for a bank.
      Here, I play video games, create mods for games, build bots, and sketch Slowpokes. I'd recommend checking out my YouTube if you're looking for some examples.
  • Q: Who edits your videos?
    • Most of my highlight videos are edited by the team at danos.works, but occasionally I'll edit one.
      I have edited all my project videos (so far!).
      If you are interested in editing for the channel, send pricing info to info@ssblur.com, along with a portfolio if you'd like.
  • Q: When do you stream?
    • I keep my stream schedule updated on my Twitch, though sometimes I will delay stream by an hour or two, since my job occasionally gets in the way.
      Additionally, sometimes I'll do additional bonus streams. When I do these, I'll announce them on my Discord
  • Q: Can I get my name in your videos?
    • Since I am generally supported well enough by sponsors, I don't have a Patreon and don't usually encourage subscriptions.
      I generally do thank and interact with regular viewers / commenters, though, so feel free to drop by my streams on Twitchor leave a comment on a video on YouTube.
  • Q: What does the name mean?
    • SSBlur is short for "Super Sonic Blur", originally stylized as SUPERSonicBlur.
      It doesn't really mean anything, I stole it from a kid when I was in elementary school. He played Webkinz and their name was "SonicBlur67" I think.
      He then suggested I should pick up Webkinz, so I did. But I had a problem: I had no idea how to pick a username.
      Well, I wanted something better than "SonicBlur".
      So I picked "SUPERSonicBlur".
